    Project Description

    During the time we will be developing our project, the focus of our team will be to solve the issue of choosing the best way to save money while doing the groceries by giving the client the necessary information to choose which supermarket has the best prices for each of the products of their list. We also thought that our app would be more complete if it also had the ability to track the expiration date of certain products and give our client the notifications not only to reduce food waste but to also help them know if they need to restock that product in the near future. If we had time, we also wanted to implement another functionality that would transfer the list our client made for a specific store in our app to their designated website so our client could make an easy purchase online without having to do the same list twice.
