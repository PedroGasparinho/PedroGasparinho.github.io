Welcome to the official CartGuru website

Our Goals

We want to help you save money by reducing food waste and buying groceries at the best prices in your local supermarkets

Our Solutions

We want to build a strong and active community, starting with you, that regularly updates the prices of products while on their trips to a supermarket. To reduce food waste we plan on sending you notifications once the expiry date of a product is close by.

Target Users

Anyone between the ages of 10-60 that wants to save money while shopping.
