About Us

Shopping for groceries can get quite expensive these days, as such our team wants to build a mobile application that helps users compare the prices of items they want to buy in nearby supermarkets, this can prevent many spontaneous decisions that added up would become costly. Food waste is also a problem our team seeks to reduce, as it was money not well spent and may encourage restocking. We want to solve this problem by tracking when groceries bought by the user expire, sending the user a notification when a product is near its expiration date.
